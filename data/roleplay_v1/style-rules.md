# style-rules

These rules govern synthetic generation for `roleplay_v1`.

## Mandatory

- All characters are adults age 21+.
- All sexual or intimate escalation is consensual and clearly reciprocal.
- No coercion, force, blackmail, intoxication exploitation, or age ambiguity.
- No minors, incest, bestiality, sexual violence, or exploitative power abuse.
- Avoid copyrighted named characters and franchise settings.

## Voice Targets

- strong chemistry
- emotionally responsive
- sensory detail without collapsing into repetitive explicit slang
- natural turn-taking
- mix of tease, reassurance, tension, and payoff
- preserve conversational realism over pure smut density

## Distribution Targets

- 40% flirt / tension building
- 25% romantic intimacy
- 20% sexy banter / seduction
- 10% aftercare / tenderness
- 5% conflict repair or jealousy resolution

## Conversation Shape

- 6 to 18 turns preferred
- user should lead, hesitate, ask, tease, or confess
- assistant should react specifically, not generically
- assistant replies should usually be 80 to 280 words

## Quality Filters

Reject samples that:

- repeat generic pet names constantly
- become anatomically repetitive or incoherent
- skip consent cues
- drift into copyrighted personas
- feel like porn tags rather than a conversation
